EXPLORE THE WILD WITH US!

VISIT ANIMALS

Discover our wide range of animal categories from all corners of the world. Explore majestic mammals like lions, tigers, and elephants in their natural-style habitats. Visit our vibrant aviary featuring colorful birds from rainforests and deserts. Dive into the underwater world with fascinating marine life like sharks, rays, and sea turtles. Get to know reptiles and amphibians, from slithering snakes to playful frogs. Every category is designed to educate and amaze visitors of all ages.

OUR CONSERVATION

Orly Zoo leads groundbreaking research in animal behavior, health, and conservation. Our team of scientists and veterinarians work to advance our understanding of wildlife.

We participate in global conservation initiatives and provide funding for field projects that protect animals in their natural habitats around the world.

ORLY ZOO CONSERVATION

Orly Zoo leads groundbreaking research in animal behavior, health, and conservation. Our team of scientists and veterinarians work to advance our understanding of wildlife.

We participate in global conservation initiatives and provide funding for field projects that protect animals in their natural habitats around the world.

THINGS TO DO

Discover a variety of exciting activities and experiences that will make your visit to Orly Zoo unforgettable.

GUIDED TOURS

Join our expert guides for an informative journey through the zoo. Learn fascinating facts about our animals and conservation efforts.

EDUCATIONAL PROGRAMS

Participate in our interactive workshops and educational sessions designed for visitors of all ages to learn about wildlife conservation.

FOOD & BEVERAGES

Enjoy a variety of dining options throughout the zoo, from quick snacks to full-service restaurants featuring local and international cuisine.

ANIMAL ENCOUNTERS

Get up close with select animals under the supervision of our trained staff. A memorable experience for animal lovers of all ages.

PHOTOGRAPHY SESSIONS

Capture stunning wildlife photos with guidance from professional photographers during our special photography sessions.

OVERNIGHT STAYS

Experience the zoo after dark with our overnight stay packages. Listen to the sounds of nocturnal animals and enjoy exclusive night tours.
