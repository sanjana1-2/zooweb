Mammal Species

African Lion

African Lions are large carnivorous mammals known for their majestic manes and social prides. They are apex predators in African savannas.

Brown beer

Brown beer, or brown ale, is a style of ale characterized by its brown color, which comes from using dark malts during brewing. It can range in flavor from sweet and malty to dry and hoppy, with flavors like caramel, chocolate, and nuts often present.

Leopard

Leopards are wildcats known for their distinctive spotted coats and strong, muscular builds. They are solitary hunters, primarily active at night, and are skilled climbers, often storing their kills in trees.

Bengal Tiger

Bengal Tigers are large carnivorous mammals with distinctive orange coats and black stripes. They inhabit forests and swamps in India and are skilled hunters.

Elephant

Elephants are the largest land mammals, known for their long trunks and large ears. They are highly intelligent and live in matriarchal herds.

Chimpanzee

Chimpanzees are great apes known for their intelligence and complex social structures. They are covered in coarse, brown or black hair, except for their bare faces, hands, and feet.
